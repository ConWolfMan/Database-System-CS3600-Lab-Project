Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

This is a student lab project built for educational purposes. It is not a commercial service. This privacy policy explains how we handle any information entered into the site.

Information We Collect

The site allows users to enter contact information such as names, phone numbers, email addresses, and physical addresses. This information is submitted through forms on the site.

Important: Do not enter real personal information into this site. It is a demonstration project and data may not be stored securely.

How We Use Information

Any information submitted is used solely for demonstrating the functionality of the phone book application as part of a course assignment. It is not sold, shared, or used for any commercial purpose.

Data Storage

Depending on the implementation stage of this project, submitted data may be stored temporarily in a database for demonstration purposes, or it may not be stored at all. Data is not retained beyond the scope of the lab project.

Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. We use Bootstrap via a CDN for styling — please refer to Bootstrap's own privacy policy for information about their data practices.

Children's Privacy

This site is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Contact

This site is a student project and is not operated by a registered business. For questions, please contact your course instructor.
