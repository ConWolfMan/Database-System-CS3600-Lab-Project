What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit.

Do We Use Cookies?

This is a student lab project created for educational purposes. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party cookies to monitor your behaviour.

Bootstrap, which we use for page styling, may load resources from a content delivery network (CDN). That CDN may set its own cookies outside of our control.

Session Cookies

We may use basic session cookies to maintain your browsing session while you navigate between pages. These are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser.

Your Control

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. Note that some parts of the site may not function properly if cookies are disabled.
